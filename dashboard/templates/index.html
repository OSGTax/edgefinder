How EdgeFinder Works

EdgeFinder is a multi-strategy paper trading arena that pits different investment philosophies against each other in real-time. Each strategy gets its own capital, makes independent decisions, and is tracked on a leaderboard.

The Arena

Two strategies compete head-to-head, each starting with $2,500 in simulated capital:

L Lynch Strategy
Growth at a reasonable price. Favors stocks with low PEG ratios, strong earnings growth, and manageable debt. Uses tighter stops (5%) and 1.5:1 reward-to-risk.
B Burry Strategy
Deep value contrarian. Targets high free cash flow yield, low price-to-tangible-book, and strong balance sheets. Wider stops (7%) and 2:1 reward-to-risk. Gets an RSI oversold boost for beaten-down names.

Data Pipeline

1 Fundamental Scanner
Nightly scan of ~8,000+ US equities via Alpaca (universe) and FMP (fundamentals). Scores each stock using Lynch and Burry criteria (50/50 weight). Top candidates go on the watchlist.
2 Signal Check (every 15 min)
Each strategy scans its watchlist for technical entry signals (RSI, MACD, EMA crossovers, volume spikes). Signals pass through a news sentiment gate before execution.
3 Execution + Risk Management
Honest executor applies realistic slippage, enforces PDT limits (3 day trades / 5 days), position sizing (2% max risk), stop losses, trailing stops, and circuit breakers. Every execution is logged with full audit trail.

Scheduler

Runs Mon-Fri during extended market hours (7 AM – 6 PM ET):

Signal check every 15 min • Position monitor every 5 min • Day trade close at 3:50 PM • Snapshots at 4:05 PM • Nightly scan at 4:30 PM

Data Sources

Alpaca — Ticker universe, real-time prices, historical bars.
FMP — Fundamental data (ratios, metrics, profiles). Cached to stay under 250/day limit.
VADER — News sentiment analysis via RSS feeds.

Dashboard Tabs

Arena — Strategy leaderboard, per-strategy positions, trade explanations, and audit trail.
Trades — All executed trades across all strategies in one view.
Watchlist — Stocks that passed fundamental screening, ranked by composite score.
Signals — Technical entry/exit signals detected on watchlist stocks.
Suggestions — Submit ideas for new features, strategy changes, or bug reports.

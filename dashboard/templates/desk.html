Before betting (paper) money on an idea, the AI tests how it would have performed in past markets versus simply buying the S&P 500.

Every night the AI tests dozens of strategy variations against 21 years of market history. A strategy makes this board only if it beat the S&P 500 in both halves of history — and its score is its performance in the WORSE half, so lucky streaks don't rank.
